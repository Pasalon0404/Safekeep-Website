SafeKeepVault Tool

Download QR Template

Download a blank, print-ready SeedQR template. Fill in the modules by hand, then scan directly into the vault.

Physical Backup Without a Computer

SeedQR is an open standard that encodes your seed phrase directly as a QR code. Rather than writing out twelve or twenty-four words, you mark a small number of modules on a pre-printed grid — a faster import process and a format that SafeKeepVault can read in a single camera scan.

Select the template that matches your seed length, download the PDF, and print it to standard 8.5×11 paper. Everything is self-contained in the file — no internet connection required to print.

  • 21 × 21 — The Compact SeedQR format for 12-word seeds. Fewer modules to fill, smaller physical footprint.
  • 25 × 25 — The Standard SeedQR format for 24-word seeds. Supports the full 256-bit entropy of a standard BIP-39 master seed.

The three pre-drawn corner squares are the QR finder patterns — they are always filled in and are included on the printed template. Mark only the data modules in the remaining area according to your seed encoding. Use a fine-tip permanent marker for clean, scannable results.
