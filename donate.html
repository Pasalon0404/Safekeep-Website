Other Ways to Contribute

SafeKeepVault is maintained by a small team. Non-financial contributions have a direct impact on the quality and reach of the project:

  • Report a bug on the GitHub issue tracker — include your hardware, OS version, and steps to reproduce.
  • Browse open issues — confirm bugs others have reported or add context to existing threads.
  • Review pull requests — read the diffs, test the changes, leave feedback.
  • Verify a signed release against the maintainer’s public key and report any discrepancy immediately.
  • Tell other Bitcoiners the project exists — word of mouth from a trusted peer is worth more than any ad.
