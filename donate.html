Why Donate

SafeKeepVault is free and open-source software. There is no company behind it, no investors, and no paid plan. Development, hardware testing, and signing infrastructure are funded entirely by donations from users.

If SafeKeepVault is useful to you and you would like to support its continued development, you can send Bitcoin to one of the addresses below. Donations cover hardware for testing, signing keys, and the maintainer's time. All donations are voluntary; the software will remain free regardless.

Other Ways to Contribute

If you cannot or do not want to donate Bitcoin, the project still benefits from any of the following:

  • Reporting bugs on the issue tracker.
  • Reviewing pull requests and reading commits.
  • Verifying signed releases against the maintainer's key.
  • Mirroring the release artifacts on your own server.
  • Telling other Bitcoiners that the project exists.
