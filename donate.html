Why Donate

SafeKeepVault is free and open-source software. There is no company behind it, no investors, and no paid plan. Development, hardware testing, and signing infrastructure are funded entirely by voluntary donations from users like you. Every sat goes directly toward hardware, signing keys, and the maintainer's time. The software will remain free regardless.

Other Ways to Contribute

If you cannot or do not want to donate Bitcoin, the project still benefits from any of the following:

  • Reporting bugs on the issue tracker.
  • Reviewing pull requests and reading commits.
  • Verifying signed releases against the maintainer's key.
  • Mirroring the release artifacts on your own server.
  • Telling other Bitcoiners that the project exists.
