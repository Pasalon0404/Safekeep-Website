The Courier

Transfer files and text between air-gapped devices using QR codes — no cables, no USB, no radio.

Launch The Courier Opens in a new tab — runs entirely in your browser.

Your Air-Gap Bridge

The Courier runs entirely in your browser — nothing is uploaded anywhere. Use the Transmit tab to encode any file or text as a QR code; point your SafeKeepVault camera at the screen and it reassembles on the other side. Use Receive to scan a QR from the vault onto your online machine, ready to broadcast or save. PSBTs in, signed transactions out, no cable required.

Set Up Your Encrypted Drive Password

The Courier has another critical use: creating the QR code that unlocks your SafeKeepVault encrypted drive on every boot. Here is the recommended workflow:

  • Generate a strong password — use your password manager. Long, fully random, saved as your primary backup.
  • Create the QR code — paste the password into “Text / password” mode on the Transmit tab. A static QR code generates instantly, locally, nothing sent anywhere.
  • Save the QR to your phone — screenshot it full-screen and store it in a locked album or your password manager’s secure notes. On every boot, hold your phone up to the vault camera to unlock instantly. No typing, no weak passwords.
