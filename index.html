SafeKeepVault

Your Hardware. Your Keys. Your Bitcoin.

Air-gapped Bitcoin signing · Zero cost of entry · Free & open-source

SafeKeepVault is an open-source Bitcoin hardware wallet and tool set that runs directly from a USB drive. By booting from your existing PC, it bypasses your computer's host OS to create a secure, air-gapped cold storage solution—providing a powerful alternative to traditional Bitcoin hardware wallets.

Repurpose, Don't Replace

By using your computer’s processor, keyboard, and full-size display, SafeKeepVault ensures every derivation path, master fingerprint, and PSBT detail is crystal clear and fully verified before you sign. This transforms your existing hardware into a multi-function Bitcoin hardware wallet, providing security and superior visibility without the need for additional devices.

  • Universal Compatibility: Runs on any x86 laptop or desktop using a standard 4GB+ USB drive. Supports most internal webcams or external cameras.
  • Hardened Isolation: Complete hardware isolation with all networking, internal SSD access, and non-essential peripherals strictly disabled upon boot.
  • Encrypted Persistence: Secure, password-protected storage featuring a streamlined, mobile-integrated unlock via QR code.

SafeKeepVault bypasses your host operating system to initialize a hardened, offline environment directly from the USB drive. By strictly isolating the system from the internet, internal storage, and unnecessary drivers, it ensures your private data never leaves the USB drive. Once the system is shut down, the memory is completely purged leaving no trace of your private keys or your activities.

Comprehensive Utility.

  • The 15-Tool Suite From generating entropy and signing transactions to sharding backups and drafting inheritance protocols, SafeKeepVault provides end-to-end Bitcoin key management.
  • Seamless Coordinator Integration Pairs natively with wallets like Sparrow, Electrum, Nunchuk, and Blue Wallet. While your software wallet tracks balances and history, SafeKeepVault serves as your dedicated, offline signing device.
  • Stateless or Persistent Architecture The choice is yours at every boot: operate in Temporary Mode, or utilize the Encrypted Vault to securely store your seed phrase for future use.
  • Optical Transmission Eliminate the need for cables or physical data swaps. Move unsigned and signed transactions via high-density QR codes, ensuring your hardware remains physically and digitally disconnected from the internet.
  • Open & Accessible SafeKeepVault is open-source, fully auditable, and free to use. There are no proprietary devices to purchase and no subscriptions to manage—simply flash a standard USB drive and secure your Bitcoin.
