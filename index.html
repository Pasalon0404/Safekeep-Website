Introducing SafeKeepVault

Your Hardware. Your Keys. Your Bitcoin.

Air-gapped Bitcoin signing · Zero cost of entry · Free & open-source

Boot any x86-based laptop or desktop computer from a USB thumb drive and SafeKeepVault turns it into a dedicated, air-gapped Bitcoin hardware wallet and utility tool. Run it 'stateless' without saving anything for a clean session, or unlock encrypted storage to permanently and securely store your keys — all on open standards, auditable code, and nothing phoning home.

Repurpose, Don't Replace

Your full-size screen means every derivation path, master fingerprint, and PSBT detail is legible before you sign it. That old device can become a pro-level signing station—no need to buy anything new.

  • 4 GB USB thumb drive or greater — any x86_64 laptop or desktop
  • No access to SSD or networking services — peripherals locked out after boot
  • Strong password-based encrypted storage, unlockable with a QR code on your phone

SafeKeepVault skips your usual OS and starts from a USB in a locked-down, offline mode. It doesn’t touch your internal drive, and once you remove the USB, everything is gone—no trace left behind.

Everything You Need. Nothing You Don't.

  • 15 tools Generate keys, sign transactions, split backups, and create inheritance instructions — the full Bitcoin key lifecycle in one place.
  • Works with your wallet Pairs with Sparrow, Electrum, Nunchuk, and Blue Wallet. Your wallet handles balances and history; SafeKeepVault handles signing.
  • Stateless or persistent Use it once and leave no trace, or save your setup and come back to it. Same drive, your choice at every boot.
  • Truly air-gapped Transfer unsigned and signed transactions via QR code — no cables, no USB swaps, no internet required.
  • Free forever Open-source and auditable. No subscription, no hardware to buy. Flash a USB drive and you’re ready to go.
