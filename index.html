SafeKeepVault

Your Hardware. Your Keys. Your Bitcoin.

Bitcoin hardware wallet · Zero cost of entry · Free & open-source

The Ultimate Bitcoin Cold Storage Device is Already on Your Desk.

SafeKeepVault is a custom-hardened Linux OS that flashes to any USB flash drive, instantly turning your existing PC into a powerful, secure Bitcoin cold storage device.

  • Open Source: Fully auditable code — every line is public and verifiable. No black boxes, no proprietary firmware.
  • Bitcoin Only: Built exclusively for Bitcoin self-custody. No altcoin distractions, no bloat.
  • True Air Gap: Network, Bluetooth, and storage interfaces are locked at the OS level before your session begins. Data passes in and out via QR code — your keys never have a path to the internet.
  • No Ecosystem Lock: Works with Sparrow, Specter, Electrum, Nunchuk, Blue Wallet, and others. Use the coordinator you already know.
  • No Blind Signing: Every transaction is fully decoded and displayed before your key is involved. You verify the destination address and amount on a full-size screen before signing is unlocked.
  • Passphrase Support: Full BIP-39 passphrase support. Add a 25th word to your seed for an additional layer of protection that makes your seed phrase useless without it.
  • Shamir & Seed XOR Backup: Split your seed into multiple shares using Shamir Secret Sharing or Seed XOR. Distribute the pieces so no single location holds your complete backup.

Repurpose, Don't Replace

If you have a PC and a 4GB flash drive, you already have everything you need. A full-size screen and keyboard make verifying transaction details effortless, and SafeKeepVault’s fifteen built-in tools handle every step of the self-custody workflow — from generating your first seed phrase to signing complex multisig transactions.

  • Universal Compatibility: Runs on any x86 laptop or desktop using a standard 4GB+ flash drive. Supports most internal webcams or external cameras.
  • Watch Wallet Integration: Works seamlessly with your favorite Bitcoin wallet software. Export your public key, build transactions in your coordinator, and sign them air-gapped using SafeKeepVault.
  • QR-Powered Unlock: Store your vault password as a QR code on your phone. Display it to the webcam on every boot to decrypt instantly — no long password to type.
  • Encrypted Storage: Safely locks away your seed phrase in a dedicated, AES-256 encrypted partition on the flash drive.

Comprehensive Utility.

  • 15-Tool Suite SafeKeepVault covers every step of the self-custody workflow — seed generation, signing, multisig coordination, encrypted backups, and more — in a single, offline environment.
  • Temporary or Encrypted The choice is yours at every boot: operate in Temporary Mode for a session that leaves no trace, or use the Encrypted Vault to store your seed securely.
  • Optical Transmission Move unsigned and signed transactions between devices via high-density QR codes — no cables, no exposed ports, no physical connection to a networked machine.
