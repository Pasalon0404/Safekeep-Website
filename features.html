What’s inside the vault

Features

Designed to keep your keys offline · Built for Bitcoin self-custody

A Fortress

Every time you boot SafeKeepVault, it's already locked down — no internet, your internal drive untouched, USB ports secured. There's nothing to configure and nothing to remember. It's just secure, every single time.

  • No internet, ever Wi-Fi and Bluetooth are shut off the moment it boots — before anything else loads. Your keys never get anywhere near the internet.
  • Truly offline Microphones and speakers are disabled too. The vault talks only to your keyboard, display, camera, and thumb drive.
  • Your computer stays clean The internal hard drive is never touched. Nothing on your computer is read, changed, or affected in any way.
  • No trace left behind Runs entirely in memory. Shut it down and everything is gone — keys, session data, clipboard history. Nothing written to your drive.
  • Plausible Deniability No special hardware, no branded device. The vault is a thumb drive that looks exactly like any other thumb drive.
  • Unlock your way Hold your phone up to the camera to scan a QR code, or type your password at the keyboard — whichever you prefer.
  • Save or start fresh Store your keys in the encrypted vault and reuse them every session, or run it 'stateless' and leave no trace. Same drive, your choice every time.

Works With the Wallets You Already Use

SafeKeepVault sits under the wallet you already use and trust. Your wallet watches your balance and history. SafeKeepVault holds your private keys and signs transactions completely offline. Send an unsigned transaction over via QR code or thumb drive, get a signed one back, and broadcast it from your everyday wallet. Your keys never touch the internet.

Doing multisig? Load your primary key and sign first. Then temporarily load a second key — from a backup share or paper wallet — and sign again. That temporary key vanishes the moment you clear it or shut down. One device, multiple signers, no extra hardware to buy.

  • Sparrow Wallet Run Sparrow on your everyday machine to watch your balance. SafeKeepVault handles signing offline. Transfer transactions via file or QR code.
  • Electrum SafeKeepVault exports a ready-to-import wallet file for Electrum and reads its QR format natively — no manual conversion needed.
  • Nunchuk Full compatibility with Nunchuk's QR scanner. Scan, broadcast, done — no extra steps.
  • Blue Wallet Import your public key into Blue Wallet to monitor your balance on your phone, then sign through SafeKeepVault before broadcasting.

What’s New in v1.1b

Full interoperability with Nunchuk and Electrum — the two strictest wallet implementations in the ecosystem. Workflows that required manual conversion steps now complete in a single scan.

v1.1b
  • Compat Flawless Nunchuk integration. SafeKeep outputs pure, un-prefixed Raw Hex QR codes. Nunchuk scans and broadcasts without any intermediate conversion step.
  • Compat Complete Electrum round-trip. A custom Base43 decoder built into SafeKeep’s camera reads Electrum’s proprietary PSBT format natively — the last manual step is gone.
  • New Electrum skeleton wallet export. SafeKeep generates a [FINGERPRINT]-electrum.json file with the correct descriptor, derivation path, and little-endian ckcc_xfp field for instant watch-only wallet creation.
