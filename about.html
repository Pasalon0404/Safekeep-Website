About SafeKeepVault

Cold Storage Without the Costs

What if the hardware you already own is all you need?

Our Mission

The mission of SafeKeepBitcoin is to make Bitcoin self-custody more accessible by lowering costs, reducing supply-chain risk, and building trustworthy, low-trust tools for sovereign ownership. We believe true ownership of money empowers individuals, and that a world where more people hold their own keys is a world with greater freedom, resilience, and sovereignty.

Our Core Principles

  • Sovereignty as a Standard — Secure self-custody Bitcoin should not carry a high barrier to entry. The tools required to protect your wealth should be free, open, and accessible to everyone.
  • Earned Trust, Not Assumed — In a world of proprietary “black box” devices, transparency is everything. Every line of our code is open-source, auditable, and verifiable.
  • Hardware Sustainability — That “old” laptop is more than enough. We believe in maximizing the utility of existing technology rather than creating more electronic waste.

A New Perspective on Cold Storage

The computer you already own is a capable Bitcoin signing device. It just needs the right software. SafeKeepVault transforms any x86 laptop or desktop into a fully air-gapped cold storage vault — bypassing the host OS, locking out all network interfaces, and running entirely from a USB drive. The result is hardware-wallet-grade security at the cost of a thumb drive, using hardware you’ve already paid for.

Free and Open Source

SafeKeepVault is released under the MIT License. Our development is public, our releases are SHA-256 verified, and our roadmap is shaped by your input.

We don’t rely on marketing claims—we rely on transparency. If you find a bug, report it. If you have an improvement, submit a pull request. The code is open for everyone to see.
